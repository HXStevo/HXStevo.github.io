🏍️ Biker's Airport Survival Guide

  • Book Meet & Greet — Hand over the keys, walk to the terminal. No bus transfers, no faff. Maximum efficiency, minimum time away from the bike.
  • Early morning flight? — Book the night before at an airport hotel with parking included. Sleep in, stroll to check-in.
  • Travelling light? — Bring your helmet bag as carry-on. Makes a surprisingly good travel bag and confuses everyone at security.
  • Return day tip — Book the latest return time slot you can. Delays happen, and you don't want parking stress on top of a delayed flight.
  • Group rides abroad? — Some car parks do multi-car deals. Get the crew to book together and split the savings.
