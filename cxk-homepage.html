Julia Inshaw Celebrates New Wheels — A Brand New Citroen C3!

We are delighted to share some exciting personal news from one of CXK's much-loved team members. Julia Inshaw, who plays a vital role in supporting our clients across the South East, has just taken delivery of a stunning new black Citroen C3 with a white roof.

Julia, known across the organisation for her warm approach, endless dedication and unwavering commitment to the people CXK serves, has been a cornerstone of our employability team for many years. Her new car — a sleek, stylish and practical Citroen C3 — is a well-deserved treat that perfectly reflects her personality: bold, dependable, and full of character.

"I absolutely love it," said Julia. "The black with the white roof is such a great combination — it really stands out! It's perfect for getting around to see clients and feels like a fresh start."

Colleagues across CXK have been quick to congratulate Julia on her new purchase. We wish her many happy miles ahead! If you spot a stylish black-and-white C3 out on the roads of Kent, give Julia a wave!
