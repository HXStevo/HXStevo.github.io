Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about booking an airport hotel in the UK.

Most airport hotel packages include overnight accommodation, free parking for the duration of your trip — usually 7 to 15 days — and a complimentary shuttle to the terminal. Many packages also include breakfast, early check-in from 6am or midnight, and late checkout options. Always check the package details before booking to confirm what is included.
We recommend booking at least two to four weeks in advance, particularly during school holidays, summer and Christmas. The earlier you book, the better the price — and availability at popular hotels fills up quickly. Last-minute deals can also be found if your dates are flexible.
Not always — it depends on the package you choose. We clearly label which packages include parking and for how many days. Hotel and parking packages typically include secured parking for 7, 8, 14 or 15 days in a monitored car park with a complimentary shuttle to the terminal. Room-only rates are available if you do not need parking.
Yes. Most airport hotels offer early check-in, sometimes from midnight for guests with very early morning flights. This is often available as an add-on or included in certain packages. We show which hotels offer early check-in in the search results so you can filter by this option.
We cover all major UK airports including London Heathrow, Gatwick, Stansted, Luton and City; Manchester, Birmingham, Edinburgh, Glasgow, Bristol, Leeds Bradford, Newcastle, Liverpool John Lennon, East Midlands, Belfast International, Belfast City, Aberdeen and Exeter. If you cannot find your airport, please contact our support team.
All car parks associated with our hotel packages are secured facilities with 24/7 CCTV monitoring, perimeter fencing, access-controlled barriers and regular patrols. Many are also Park Mark accredited — the Safer Parking Award given to car parks that meet standards set by the British Parking Association and the police.
Most of our hotel bookings offer free cancellation up to 24 or 48 hours before your check-in date. The exact cancellation policy varies by hotel and is shown clearly before you complete your booking. To amend your dates or add extras, contact our UK customer service team who are available 24 hours a day.
