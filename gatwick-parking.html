North vs South Terminal

✈️ North Terminal

  • Airlines: easyJet, TUI, Norse Atlantic, others
  • Parking: Short Stay North, Long Stay North
  • Meet & Greet: Available directly outside terminal
  • Shuttle: Free monorail to South Terminal
  • Tip: Check your boarding pass — always confirm your terminal before you go

✈️ South Terminal

  • Airlines: British Airways, Vueling, Volotea, others
  • Parking: Short Stay South, Long Stay South
  • Meet & Greet: Drop-off zone on forecourt level
  • Shuttle: Free monorail to North Terminal
  • Tip: If in doubt, both terminals are connected — 3 min monorail ride

💡 Gatwick Parking Tips

  • Book as early as possible Prices can be up to 60% cheaper when booked weeks in advance vs turning up on the day. Don't leave it late.
  • Always check which terminal you're flying from Gatwick has two terminals — North and South. They're connected by a free monorail but wrong terminal = stress you don't need.
  • Meet & Greet is worth it for early flights If you've got a 6am departure, driving straight to the terminal and handing over the keys saves serious time and hassle vs shuttle buses.
  • Build in transfer time for Park & Ride Off-site shuttle buses run every 10–15 minutes. Add at least 30–40 minutes to your plan so you're not rushing.
  • Compare on-airport vs off-airport On-airport is convenient but off-site operators are often just as close and significantly cheaper — especially for trips over 5 days.
  • Take a photo of your parking space Simple but often forgotten. After a holiday, trying to remember Bay 3F Level 2 at 11pm is nobody's idea of fun.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book Gatwick parking?

As early as possible — ideally 4–8 weeks before you fly. The earlier you book, the cheaper it is. Last-minute prices at Gatwick can be 3–4x more expensive than pre-booked rates.

What's the cheapest parking option at Gatwick?

Off-airport Park & Ride is typically the cheapest option, often starting from around £25 for a week. Long Stay parking is the best value for on-airport if you prefer to park closer.

Is Meet & Greet safe at Gatwick?

Yes — reputable Meet & Greet operators at Gatwick are vetted and insured. Always book through a trusted provider like Holiday Extras rather than unbranded services.

Can I change or cancel my parking booking?

Most pre-booked parking includes free cancellation up to 24–48 hours before arrival, especially when booked through Holiday Extras. Always check the terms before booking.

How early should I arrive at Gatwick?

Allow at least 2 hours before a short-haul flight and 3 hours for long-haul. Add 30–45 minutes if using off-site Park & Ride, and factor in security queue times during peak periods.

Does Gatwick have electric vehicle charging?

Yes — Gatwick's Long Stay and Short Stay car parks both have EV charging points. It's worth calling ahead or checking availability before you go, especially during busy periods.
