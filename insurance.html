Eligibility Criteria

You must

  • Live in the UK, the Channel Islands or the Isle of Man and have lived there for at least 6 of the last 12 months
  • Be registered with a General Practitioner in that location
  • Follow the latest Foreign, Commonwealth and Development Office (FCDO) travel advice
  • Start and end your trip in the UK
  • Be in the UK when you buy your policy

When cover doesn't apply:

  • If buying insurance with the intention of making a claim
  • If you are already aware of something that's likely to lead to a claim
  • If you are travelling against medical advice, or travelling specifically to receive medical treatment
